To whomever is unfortunate enough to have reached this page after frantically clicking every [GENERIC BROWSER ENGINE] result:

I am sorry to inform you that I have no prior experience in game development. ESPECIALLY 3D game development.

Realistically, I have been a software engineer for about 5 years, writing HTML on top of HTML on top of legacy services, and all of that has become quite boring to me. So, the only conclusion I could come up with was that I needed to start playing video games, AGAIN, to relive my childhood bliss. I played Valheim for hours on end, which is a masterpiece of a game. This was the catalyst which made me wonder if I could make a cool game that people would write silly blogs about.

The game I'm going to be developing is called `TURRIS` , which is the latin word for the tower in a church. The end-goal is to be able to create a single/multi player game, with a cool fighting abilities, mazes, puzzles, and an intricate power system.

The next pages of the blog will just be tracking my journey in the world of game development, showcasing all the little interesting tidbits of information I find along the way.

Also, I am developing this in [Bevy](https://bevy.org/).
